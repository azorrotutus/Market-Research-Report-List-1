<p><p>The orange juice market comprises several types. </p><p>1. **100% Fruit Juice** contains no added sugars or preservatives, offering pure orange flavor.   </p><p>2. **Nectars** typically blend fruit juice with water and sweeteners, resulting in a richer taste and thicker consistency.  </p><p>3. **Juice Drinks** are often diluted with water and loaded with added sugars, making them more affordable but less nutritious.  </p><p>4. **Concentrates** involve removing water from juice for easier transport, requiring reconstitution before consumption.  </p><p>5. **Powdered Juice** is dehydrated and mixed with water to create a liquid form, offering convenience and long shelf life.</p></p>
