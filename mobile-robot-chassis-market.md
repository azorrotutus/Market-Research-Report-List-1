<p><p>The mobile robot chassis market is primarily divided into two types: tracked and wheeled mobile robot chassis. Tracked mobile robot chassis utilize continuous tracks for improved mobility and traction on uneven terrain, making them ideal for applications in rugged environments. In contrast, wheeled mobile robot chassis are equipped with wheels, offering faster speeds and simpler navigation on flat surfaces, making them suitable for indoor applications and environments with smooth terrains. Both types are essential for diverse robotic applications across various industries.</p></p>
