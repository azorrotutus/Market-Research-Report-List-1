<p><p>Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) involves techniques used to increase the amount of crude oil that can be extracted from an oil field. The main types include gas injection, where gases like CO2 are injected to maintain reservoir pressure; thermal injection, which involves steam injection to reduce oil viscosity; and chemical injection, where polymers or surfactants are introduced to improve flow characteristics. Each method aims to optimize extraction efficiency and prolong the productive life of oil reservoirs while reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
