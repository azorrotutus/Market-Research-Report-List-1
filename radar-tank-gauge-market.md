<p><ul><li>Non-contact Systems</li><li>Contact Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radar Tank Gauge market is divided into two primary types: non-contact and contact systems. Non-contact systems utilize radar waves to measure liquid levels without physically touching the substance, offering advantages like reduced maintenance and compatibility with various media. In contrast, contact systems involve direct measurement sensors that come into contact with the liquid, providing precise readings but may require more frequent maintenance. Both types find applications in industries such as oil and gas, chemicals, and water management.</p></p>
