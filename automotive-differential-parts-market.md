<p><p>The automotive differential parts market includes several critical components. Pinion bearings support the pinion shaft, ensuring smooth rotation. Spider and side gears facilitate torque distribution between drive wheels. Side bearings stabilize the differential assembly, minimizing wear. The differential case houses and protects internal components while allowing gear engagement. Differential drive gears transmit power from the drive shaft to the wheels. Other parts may include seals and shims, essential for maintaining operational efficiency and longevity of the differential system.</p></p>
