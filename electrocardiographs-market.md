<p><strong>The Electrocardiographs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resting System</li><li>Stress System</li><li>Holter Monitor System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrocardiograph market comprises various types, including resting systems, stress systems, and Holter monitor systems. Resting systems are designed for standard ECG recordings at rest, providing a baseline heart activity analysis. Stress systems assess cardiac response during physical exertion, often used in exercise testing. Holter monitor systems offer continuous ECG monitoring over 24 to 48 hours, capturing irregularities in heart rhythms during daily activities. Each system serves specific diagnostic needs in cardiology, enhancing patient evaluation and treatment planning.</p></p>
