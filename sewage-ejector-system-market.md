<p><p>The sewage ejector system market is categorized into three main types: submersible, vertical, and self-priming systems. Submersible ejectors are designed to operate underwater, effectively pumping wastewater from basements or lower levels. Vertical ejectors are mounted above ground and suited for larger applications, offering high efficiency. Self-priming systems can lift sewage from lower levels without requiring a submerged pump, making them versatile for various installations. Each type serves specific operational needs and installation environments in wastewater management.</p></p>
