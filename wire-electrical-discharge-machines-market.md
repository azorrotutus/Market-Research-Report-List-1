Flush Type EDMs use a dielectric fluid that is circulated through the cutting area, enhancing flushing efficiency and improving machining precision. Submerged Type EDMs operate with the workpiece fully submerged in dielectric fluid, offering better cooling and minimal wire wear. Other types may include specialized or hybrid machines catering to unique industrial needs, contributing to diverse applications in precision machining.</p></p>
