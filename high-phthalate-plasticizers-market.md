<p><p>The high phthalate plasticizers market includes several key types: DINP (Diisononyl phthalate), known for its high performance in polyvinyl chloride (PVC) applications; DIDP (Diisodecyl phthalate), utilized for its thermal stability and low volatility; DPHP (Diphenyl propane, also known as DPHP), appreciated for its flexibility and durability; DIUP (Diisoundecyl phthalate) and DTDP (Dibutylnonyl phthalate), both recognized for enhancing product longevity and flexibility. These plasticizers are primarily used in various industrial applications, including construction and automotive industries.</p></p>
