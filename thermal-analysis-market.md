<p><p>The thermal analysis market encompasses various analytical techniques used to study material properties as they change with temperature. Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC) measures heat flow variations to assess thermal transitions. Thermogravimetric Analysis (TGA) monitors weight changes with temperature. Simultaneous Thermal Analyzers combine DSC and TGA functionalities. Differential Thermal Analyzers (DTA) evaluate temperature differences between a sample and a reference. Thermomechanical Analyzers measure dimensional changes, while Dynamic Mechanical Analyzers assess mechanical properties under varying thermal conditions. Other market segments may include specialized or emerging techniques.</p></p>
