<p><ul><li>Activated Carbon Vapor Traps</li><li>Cold Vapor Traps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vapor traps market primarily consists of two types: activated carbon vapor traps and cold vapor traps. Activated carbon vapor traps utilize activated carbon to adsorb volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and other pollutants from gases, making them effective in various industrial applications. Conversely, cold vapor traps operate by cooling vapors to condense and capture them, preventing emissions of hazardous substances. Both types play essential roles in environmental protection and compliance with regulatory standards in various industries.</p></p>
