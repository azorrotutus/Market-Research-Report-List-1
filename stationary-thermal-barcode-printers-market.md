<p><strong>The Stationary Thermal Barcode Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Thermal</li><li>Thermal Transfer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary thermal barcode printers use two primary technologies: direct thermal and thermal transfer. Direct thermal printers create images by applying heat to specialized heat-sensitive paper, resulting in a quick, cost-effective solution for short-term applications like shipping labels. In contrast, thermal transfer printers utilize a heat process to transfer ink from a ribbon onto various materials, providing more durable and long-lasting prints suitable for environments where barcode longevity is essential, such as in inventory management or industrial applications.</p></p>
