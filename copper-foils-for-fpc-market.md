<p><p>The copper foils for flexible printed circuit (FPC) market primarily consists of two types: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil is produced through an electrochemical process, resulting in a fine, uniform thickness ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, rolled copper foil is manufactured by mechanically rolling copper sheets, offering cost-effectiveness and versatility for various applications. Both types play crucial roles in the electronics industry, catering to the evolving demands for lightweight and flexible circuit solutions.</p></p>
