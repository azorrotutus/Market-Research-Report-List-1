<p><p>The Positron Emission Tomography (PET) scanner market is primarily divided into two types: full-ring and partial-ring PET scanners. Full-ring PET scanners feature a complete circular array of detectors, providing enhanced image quality and uniformity, making them suitable for detailed anatomical and functional imaging. In contrast, partial-ring PET scanners have a limited detection ring, typically offering more compact designs and lower costs, suitable for specific applications or smaller facilities where space and budget are constraints.</p></p>
