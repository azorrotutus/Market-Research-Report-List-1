<p><p>Non-toluene inks for flexible packaging utilize alternative solvents like ethyl acetate and n-butyl acetate, which are less harmful and more environmentally friendly than traditional solvent-based inks. Ethyl acetate is known for its quick drying properties and excellent adhesion, making it suitable for high-speed printing applications. N-butyl acetate offers a balance of drying time and print durability, making it an attractive option for various substrates. Both options enhance safety while maintaining print quality, catering to the growing demand for sustainable packaging solutions.</p></p>
