<p><ul><li>Serum Separating Tubes</li><li>EDTA Tubes</li><li>Plasma Separation Tubes</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum blood tubes market comprises various types, each serving specific laboratory needs. Serum separating tubes facilitate the separation of serum from clotting blood, enabling efficient biochemical testing. EDTA tubes are designed for hematology tests, preserving blood cellular components by preventing coagulation. Plasma separation tubes are used for obtaining plasma quickly through centrifugation, crucial for certain assays. Other tubes include those for specialized tests, such as blood cultures or specific additive formulations, catering to diverse diagnostic requirements in clinical settings.</p></p>
