<p><strong>The PV Junction Box Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potting PV Junction Box</li><li>Non-Potting PV Junction Box</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PV junction box market is categorized into two main types: potting and non-potting junction boxes. Potting PV junction boxes are encased in a protective resin, enhancing durability and moisture resistance, making them suitable for harsh outdoor conditions. In contrast, non-potting PV junction boxes lack this encapsulation, offering a lighter and more cost-effective solution, although with reduced protection from environmental factors. The choice between the two depends on the specific application requirements and environmental challenges faced by solar systems.</p></p>
