<p><p>The non-contact temperature measurement sensor market features two primary types: thermopile infrared (IR) sensors and laser temperature sensors. Thermopile IR sensors detect infrared radiation emitted by objects to measure temperature, making them suitable for applications like HVAC and medical diagnostics. Laser temperature sensors, on the other hand, use focused laser beams to enhance measurement accuracy from a distance, ideal for industrial processes. Both technologies enable quick, accurate readings without physical contact, improving safety and efficiency across various sectors.</p></p>
