<p><p>The pharmaceutical filling machine market is primarily divided into two types: semi-automatic and fully automatic filling machines. Semi-automatic filling machines require manual intervention for operation and are ideal for small to medium-sized production, offering flexibility and lower initial costs. In contrast, fully automatic filling machines operate independently, ensuring high-speed, efficiency, and precision for large-scale production. They minimize human error and are designed for continuous operation, making them suitable for high-volume pharmaceutical manufacturing environments.</p></p>
