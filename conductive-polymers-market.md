<p><strong>The Conductive Polymers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrically Conducting Polymers</li><li>Thermally Conducting Polymers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conductive polymers market comprises two main types: electrically conducting polymers and thermally conducting polymers. Electrically conducting polymers, such as polyaniline and polypyrrole, are used in applications like flexible electronics and sensors due to their ability to transport electricity. Thermally conducting polymers, on the other hand, are designed to efficiently conduct heat, making them suitable for applications in thermal management and electronic devices. Both types are integral in advancing technologies across various industries, including electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
