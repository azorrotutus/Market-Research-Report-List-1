<p><p>Bearing pullers are categorized into several types based on their operation and mechanism. Hydraulic pullers utilize fluid pressure for efficient and powerful extraction of bearings, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Mechanical pullers employ a screw mechanism that requires manual effort to pull bearings, often used for smaller, lighter tasks. Manual pullers are simple handheld tools designed for quick and easy bearing removal without complex mechanisms. Other types may include specialized or hybrid pullers, tailored for specific industry needs.</p></p>
