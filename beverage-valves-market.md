<p><p>The beverage valves market comprises two main types: plastic valves and metal valves. Plastic valves are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and often preferred for low-pressure applications and environments where sanitation is crucial. They are widely used in packaging and dispensing systems for juices and soft drinks. In contrast, metal valves, known for their durability and ability to handle higher pressures, are commonly used in brewing and carbonated beverage systems. Both types cater to specific industry needs based on performance, cost, and application requirements.</p></p>
