<p><strong>The Nano Positioning Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Sensor</li><li>Piezoresistive Sensor</li><li>Piezoelectric Sensor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nano Positioning Systems (NPS) utilize various sensor types for precise positioning at the nanoscale. Capacitive sensors measure changes in capacitance to detect position shifts, offering high resolution and stability. Piezoresistive sensors exploit resistance changes in materials under stress, providing accurate readings for dynamic applications. Piezoelectric sensors convert mechanical stress into electrical signals, enabling rapid and precise movements. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses specialized sensors like optical and inductive types that cater to niche applications, expanding the NPS market further.</p></p>
