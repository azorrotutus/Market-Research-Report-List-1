<p><p>The Employer of Record (EOR) platform market features two primary models: the Aggregator Model and the Wholly Owned Infrastructure Model. In the Aggregator Model, a central platform collaborates with multiple local EOR providers, facilitating employment services across various regions without owning the infrastructure. Conversely, the Wholly Owned Infrastructure Model entails a single EOR provider fully controlling its operations, compliance, and payroll systems, allowing for a more integrated and streamlined approach to workforce management and employment services in diverse markets.</p></p>
