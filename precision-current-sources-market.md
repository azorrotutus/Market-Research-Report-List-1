<p><p>The precision current sources market is divided into two primary types: unipolar and bipolar current sources. Unipolar current sources provide a single polarity output, often used in applications requiring consistent current flow in one direction, like LED drivers. Conversely, bipolar current sources supply both positive and negative current outputs, making them ideal for applications where bidirectional current flow is essential, such as in testing or dynamic signal generation. Both types are crucial in various industries, including electronics and telecommunications.</p></p>
