<p><p>The sandwich panel press market can be categorized into two main types: discontinuous and continuous. Discontinuous systems involve batch processing, where panels are produced in separate cycles, allowing flexibility for varied panel designs and sizes. Continuous systems, on the other hand, operate in a streamlined fashion, producing panels continuously for high-volume output. This approach enhances efficiency and reduces production time, making it ideal for large-scale manufacturing. Each type serves distinct industry needs based on production scale and operational efficiency.</p></p>
