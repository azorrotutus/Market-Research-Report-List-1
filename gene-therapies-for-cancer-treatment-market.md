<p><strong>The Gene Therapies for Cancer Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Somatic Cell Gene Therapy (SCGT)</li><li>Germline Gene Therapy (GGT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gene therapies for cancer treatment can be classified into two main types: Somatic Cell Gene Therapy (SCGT) and Germline Gene Therapy (GGT). SCGT targets and modifies the genetic material of somatic cells, aiming to treat cancer by enhancing the immune response or correcting genetic defects within the tumor. In contrast, GGT involves altering the genes in germline cells, potentially affecting future generations, and is primarily focused on preventing hereditary cancers. Both approaches hold promise for personalized cancer treatment.</p></p>
