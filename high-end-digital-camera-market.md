<p><p>The high-end digital camera market comprises several types. DSLR cameras offer versatile lens options and optical viewfinders, catering to professional photographers. Mirrorless cameras provide a lighter alternative, with electronic viewfinders and interchangeable lenses, appealing to enthusiasts. Bridge cameras blend compactness with advanced features, resembling DSLRs but lacking detachable lenses, ideal for casual users. Compact digital cameras prioritize portability and ease of use, often with fixed lenses, serving those who seek convenience and simplicity without sacrificing image quality.</p></p>
