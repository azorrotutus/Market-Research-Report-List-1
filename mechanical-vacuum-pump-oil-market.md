<p><p>The mechanical vacuum pump oil market is primarily classified into two main types: semi-synthetic vacuum pump oil and fully synthetic vacuum pump oil. Semi-synthetic oils combine mineral oil with synthetic additives, offering improved thermal stability and reduced evaporation losses. Fully synthetic vacuum pump oils, on the other hand, are entirely formulated from synthetic base stocks, providing superior performance, enhanced lubrication, and longer service life in demanding conditions. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, improving efficiency and reliability in vacuum systems.</p></p>
