<p><ul><li>Anion Ion-exchange Polymer</li><li>Cation Ion-exchange Polymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ion-exchange polymer market is primarily categorized into anion and cation ion-exchange polymers. Anion ion-exchange polymers are designed to remove positively charged ions from solutions, making them ideal for applications like water purification and chemical processing. Conversely, cation ion-exchange polymers target negatively charged ions, effectively facilitating processes such as softening hard water and removing heavy metals. Both types play crucial roles in industries like pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and wastewater treatment, enhancing water quality and process efficiency.</p></p>
