<p><p>The power transformers market primarily comprises two types: dry-type transformers and oil-immersed transformers. Dry-type transformers use air for cooling and are often preferred in indoor applications due to their safety and low maintenance requirements. They are suitable for environments where fire risk is a concern. On the other hand, oil-immersed transformers utilize oil for cooling and insulation, making them ideal for high-capacity and outdoor applications. Each type serves distinct market needs based on location, safety concerns, and energy requirements.</p></p>
