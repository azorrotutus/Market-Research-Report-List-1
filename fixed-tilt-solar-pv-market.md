<p><p>The fixed tilt solar photovoltaic (PV) market consists of three main types: Mono-Si (monocrystalline), Multi-Si (polycrystalline), and Thin Film. Mono-Si panels offer high efficiency and space-saving designs, making them ideal for limited areas. Multi-Si panels are cost-effective and widely used but generally have lower efficiency. Thin Film technology provides flexibility and lightweight solutions, suitable for various applications but typically less efficient than crystalline types. Together, these options cater to diverse installation needs and budget considerations.</p></p>
