<p><p>The open motor yachts market features two primary hull types: planing and displacement. Planing hulls are designed for speed and agility, allowing the yacht to rise above the water at high speeds, ideal for those seeking quick travel and sporty performance. In contrast, displacement hulls prioritize stability and comfort, moving through the water at lower speeds while providing a smoother ride. Each type caters to different preferences, with planing yachts appealing to thrill-seekers and displacement yachts attracting those valuing leisurely cruising.</p></p>
