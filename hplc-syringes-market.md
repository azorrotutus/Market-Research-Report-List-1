<p><p>The HPLC syringes market is divided into two primary types: autosampler syringes and manual syringes. Autosampler syringes are designed for automated liquid sampling in high-throughput environments, improving efficiency and accuracy in analytical processes. They typically feature consistent volume delivery and compatibility with various autosampler systems. In contrast, manual syringes offer flexibility for smaller-scale applications or specific sample preparations, allowing for controlled manual injection and handling, making them suitable for diverse laboratory settings.</p></p>
