<p><p>Organic Photovoltaics (OPV) harness organic materials for solar energy conversion, offering flexibility and lightweight features. The market types include P-N junction structures, specifically P-N heterojunctions, which combine donor and acceptor materials for improved charge separation and efficiency. Additionally, dye-sensitized nanocrystalline solar cells (DSSC) utilize a photosensitive dye to absorb sunlight and generate electrons, with a porous semiconductor layer to facilitate electron transport. Both technologies emphasize low-cost production and adaptability for various applications in the renewable energy sector.</p></p>
