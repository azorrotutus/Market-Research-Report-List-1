<p><p>The superheaters market is categorized into three main types: Radiant Superheaters, which utilize the radiant heat from combustion gases to raise steam temperature; Convection Superheaters, that rely on the convective heat transfer from hot gases to steam; and Combined Superheaters, which integrate both radiant and convective heating methods for improved efficiency. Each type serves distinct applications in power generation and industrial processes, optimizing steam quality and enhancing overall system performance. These variations cater to diverse operational needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
