<p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) system market comprises two main types: automatic CPAP and non-automatic CPAP. Automatic CPAP devices adjust pressure levels in real-time based on a patient's breathing patterns, providing personalized treatment and enhancing comfort. In contrast, non-automatic CPAP devices deliver a fixed pressure throughout the night, requiring users to set the pressure themselves. These variations cater to different patient needs, with automatic systems typically preferred for their adaptability, while non-automatic options remain cost-effective and straightforward.</p></p>
