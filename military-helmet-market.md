<p><p>The military helmet market is categorized into three main types based on material: metal, nonmetal, and composite materials. Metal helmets, often made from steel or aluminum, offer durability and protection but can be heavier. Nonmetal materials, such as plastics or fibers, provide lightweight options while maintaining some protective features. Composite materials, which combine various components like aramid fibers and resins, achieve a balance of strength, weight, and flexibility, making them increasingly popular for modern military applications.</p></p>
