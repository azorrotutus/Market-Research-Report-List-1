<p><strong>The Flange Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integral Flange</li><li>Threaded Flange</li><li>Butt Welding Flange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flange market encompasses various types, including integral, threaded, and butt welding flanges. Integral flanges are formed as a single piece with the pipe, ensuring high strength and reliability. Threaded flanges have internal threads for easy installation and removal, suitable for low-pressure applications. Butt welding flanges allow pipes to be welded directly, providing a strong, seamless connection, ideal for high-pressure systems. Each type serves distinct needs in industries such as oil, gas, and construction, contributing to the overall flange market's growth.</p></p>
