<p><ul><li>Vane Anemometer</li><li>Cup Anemometer</li><li>Thermal Anemometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic anemometer market comprises several types, including vane, cup, and thermal anemometers. Vane anemometers use rotating blades to measure wind speed, making them suitable for indoor and outdoor applications. Cup anemometers consist of rotating cups to capture wind velocity, commonly used in meteorology and wind energy assessments. Thermal anemometers measure airflow by detecting changes in temperature, offering precision in low-velocity environments. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to diverse industries such as meteorology, HVAC, and wind energy.</p></p>
