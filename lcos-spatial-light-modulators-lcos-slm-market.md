<p><p>LCoS Spatial Light Modulators (LCOS-SLM) are devices that use liquid crystal technology to manipulate light for various applications, including projection, imaging, and holography. The market is divided into two main types: reflective LCOS-SLM, which reflects light off a surface, enhancing contrast and brightness, making it ideal for high-resolution displays; and transmissive LCOS-SLM, which transmits light through the liquid crystals, suitable for applications requiring high precision and uniformity in light modulation. Both types cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
