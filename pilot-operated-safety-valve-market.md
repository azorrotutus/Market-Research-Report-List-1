<p><strong>The Pilot Operated Safety Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Type</li><li>Non-closed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pilot Operated Safety Valve (POSV) market is categorized into closed and non-closed types. Closed type POSVs operate continuously in a closed position, maintaining pressure until a predetermined level is exceeded, ensuring minimal leakage. In contrast, non-closed type POSVs can release pressure without being fully closed, offering rapid response to overpressure situations. Both types are critical in various industries for maintaining safety and equipment integrity, with applications in chemical, oil and gas, and power generation sectors.</p></p>
