<p><ul><li>Internal Installation</li><li>External Installation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DC Optimizers are used in two primary market types: internal and external installation. Internal installations refer to systems integrated within solar panels or modules, optimizing performance on-site. These optimizers enhance energy output, monitor individual panel performance, and improve shading issues. External installations, on the other hand, are standalone devices located outside the panels, serving multiple modules. This setup allows for flexibility in system design, facilitating upgrades and maintenance while optimizing energy production across various configurations.</p></p>
