<p><p>The beverage dispenser market is primarily divided into two types: countertop dispensers and floor-standing dispensers. Countertop dispensers are compact, designed for smaller spaces, making them ideal for cafes and small restaurants. They are user-friendly and suitable for quick service. In contrast, floor-standing dispensers are larger and intended for high-capacity service environments such as buffets and large venues. These dispensers often feature multiple beverage options and are designed for heavy use, offering convenience and efficiency in serving beverages.</p></p>
