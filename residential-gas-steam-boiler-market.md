<p><p>The residential gas steam boiler market is divided into two primary types: multiple steam units and single steam units. Multiple steam units consist of interconnected boilers that can collectively meet higher steam demands and offer improved efficiency and redundancy. In contrast, single steam units are standalone systems designed for individual household needs, ideal for smaller homes or specific applications. Each type caters to different residential requirements, with multiple units providing scalability and resilience, while single units focus on simplicity and ease of installation.</p></p>
