<p><strong>The Distributed Strain Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Based on the Rayleigh Backscatter Deliver</li><li>Based on Fiber Bragg Gratings (FBGs) Deliver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Distributed Strain Sensor market can be segmented based on two key technologies: Rayleigh Backscatter and Fiber Bragg Gratings (FBGs). Rayleigh Backscatter sensors utilize the scattering of light within optical fibers to detect strain along the fiber length, offering high spatial resolution and continuous monitoring. In contrast, FBGs employ periodic variations in the refractive index of the fiber to create discrete sensing points, allowing for precise measurement of strain and temperature at specific locations. Both technologies cater to various applications across industries such as civil engineering, oil and gas, and aerospace.</p></p>
