<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Steam Turbine MRO Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial</li><li>Radial</li><li>Mixed</li><li>Tangential or Helical</li><li>Reentry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Steam Turbine MRO market is categorized into several types based on design and operational characteristics. Axial turbines feature a design where steam flows parallel to the turbine shaft, enhancing efficiency. Radial turbines have steam flowing perpendicular, offering compactness. Mixed turbines combine features of both axial and radial designs for versatility. Tangential or helical turbines direct steam tangentially to the rotor, optimizing performance. Reentry turbines allow steam to flow back into the turbine to improve energy extraction and efficiency.</p></p>
