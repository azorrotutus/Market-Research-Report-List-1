<p><ul><li>Single-Beam UV-Vis Spectrophotometers</li><li>Double-Beam UV-Vis Spectrophotometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV-Visible spectrometer market is primarily categorized into single-beam and double-beam UV-Vis spectrophotometers. Single-beam UV-Vis spectrophotometers measure absorbance by directing light through the sample and use a reference measurement taken before analysis. They are generally simpler and more affordable. In contrast, double-beam UV-Vis spectrophotometers split the light path, allowing simultaneous measurement of the sample and a reference, enhancing accuracy and stability. The choice between them depends on the required precision, application, and budget considerations.</p></p>
