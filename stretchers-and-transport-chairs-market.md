<p><p>The stretchers and transport chairs market comprises three main types: manual, electric, and hydraulic. Manual stretchers require human effort for movement and adjustment, offering simplicity and ease of use. Electric stretchers feature powered systems for effortless height and position adjustments, enhancing patient comfort and caregiver efficiency. Hydraulic stretchers utilize a hydraulic mechanism for smooth height modifications, providing stability and ease during transport. Each type serves distinct needs in healthcare settings, catering to different patient care and mobility requirements.</p></p>
